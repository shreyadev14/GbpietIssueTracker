“Best way to escape from your problems is to solve them.”

This project is made to provide a platform for the people of our organization to share the issues they are facing so that they can be resolved by a collaborative effort of all the entities belonging to the same organization.

The extent of this project is the problem base itself.It will be outdated the day people will stop facing problems .The goal is to traverse the gap between the entity facing the problem and the one solving it while smoothing the process.
